E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)
This project presents a full end-to-end analysis of an e-commerce dataset, delivered as part of my Data Science internship at Future Interns. The Power BI report explores regional performance, product insights, category trends, and sales behavior using interactive dashboards and custom DAX metrics.

Key Insights
Regional Performance
- The Western region consistently leads in sales and quantity.
- The East and Central regions follow, while the South lags except in profit, where it slightly surpasses Central.

Product Performance
- Top-selling product by ID: TEC-CO-10004722 with 62K+ total sales.
- Product OFF-BI-10003527 follows at 27K+ sales.
- OFF-BI (office supply) products receive the highest discounts, appearing in 4 of the top 10 most sold items.
- KPIs:
    - Total Sales: 2.3M
    - Total Quantity: 38K
    - Total Discount: 1.56K

Sales Trends (2011-2014)
- Sales peaks occur consistently in March, September, and December.
- Recommendations: Ensure high stock availability, especially technology products, during these periods to maximize revenue.

Category Insights
- Technology leads in both sales and profit, generating over 50% of total profit.
- Office Supplies records the highest order quantities, likely driven by effective discount strategies.
- Furniture contributes the smallest profit share.

General Dashboard Summary
- Western region tops with 250K+ sales; South is lowest at <123K.
- Strong correlation between heavy discounts and high product movement, especially within Office Supplies.
- Custom DAX calculations show:
    - Average Order Value (AOV): 433.77
    - Repeat Customer Rate: 71%
